To do a burpee
, start standing, squat down, place hands on the floor, kick feet back to a plank, do a push-up (optional), jump feet forward, then jump up explosively, landing softly to repeat. Focus on keeping your core tight, back straight, and engaging your whole body for this full-body cardio and strength move, with variations available for all fitness levels.
